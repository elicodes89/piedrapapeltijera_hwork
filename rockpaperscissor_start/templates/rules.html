Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

✂️ Players start each round by saying, “rock, paper, scissors, shoot!”
🪨 On “shoot,” each player holds out their fist for rock, flat hand for paper, or their index and middle finger for scissors.
🧻 Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock.
